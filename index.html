June Highlights & Acknowledgements

Successful June Activities

The June activities including Madaraka Day, Father's Day soccer match, and World Environment Day were excellent opportunities for social media content creation. These events:

  • Generated authentic, engaging content that resonated with our audience
  • Showcased our company culture and team spirit
  • Provided diverse content types (photos, videos, stories)
  • Increased organic reach through staff sharing

Recommendation: The hotel should consider more such team activities as they provide excellent social media content opportunities while boosting staff morale.

Team Restructuring

In June, I transitioned from Sales to join Corretta, whose sharp eye for content and passion for social media have elevated our digital marketing. Her leadership has already boosted our content quality and engagement.

Resource Allocation

We received KES 39,000 on June 27 to support our digital marketing efforts. This funding will be used for:

  • Metricool monthly subscription for social media management
  • Adobe Creative Suite for enhanced content creation
  • Strategic boosting of high-performing posts

Note: Since this funding arrived late in the month (June 27), its full impact will be realized in July.

Special Thanks

We appreciate management's support in securing essential resources and creating a positive work environment. The team-building activities in June were particularly valuable for morale and provided excellent content opportunities. We look forward to implementing these resources in July to drive even better results.
